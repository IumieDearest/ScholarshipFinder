# 🎓 ScholarshipFinder
### A Centralized Portal for Institutional Scholarship Discovery and Management in Cebu

> 💸 Tired of missing scholarship deadlines because the info is scattered everywhere? **ScholarshipFinder** is a centralized portal built for students in Cebu that brings all scholarship opportunities — from universities, LGUs, and NGOs — into one place. Apply, track, and never miss a deadline again.

---

## 📌 About the Project

**ScholarshipFinder** is a capstone project developed by students of the **College of Computer Studies, University of Southern Philippines Foundation**. It addresses the widespread problem of scholarship information asymmetry and administrative fragmentation experienced by students and scholarship providers in Cebu City.

Instead of hunting through bulletin boards, Facebook groups, and scattered websites, students can now search, apply, and track all their scholarship applications from a single, centralized web portal.

---

## 🚩 The Problem

- 📋 Scholarship listings are fragmented across multiple platforms
- ⏰ Students miss deadlines due to disorganized information
- 😓 Providers rely on manual spreadsheets and email-heavy communication
- 📍 No existing platform serves the specific needs of Cebu's regional scholarship ecosystem

---

## ✅ The Solution

A **multi-tenant, web-based scholarship management portal** that serves as a single source of truth for both students and scholarship providers in Cebu City.

---

## ✨ Features

### 👩‍🎓 For Students (Incoming & Currently Enrolled College Students)
- 🔍 **Scholarship Search & Discovery** — Browse consolidated listings from universities, LGUs, and NGOs
- 🤖 **Profile-Based Matching** — Get personalized scholarship recommendations based on your academic profile
- 📝 **Online Application Submission** — Apply directly through the portal
- 📂 **Document Upload & Management** — Submit and manage required files in one place
- 📊 **Real-Time Application Tracking** — Monitor your application status at every stage
- 🔔 **Automated Deadline Reminders** — Never miss an important date again

### 🏢 For Scholarship Providers (Universities, LGUs, NGOs)
- 📋 **Admin Dashboard** — Manage all applications from one place
- ⚙️ **Automated Applicant Filtering** — Quickly shortlist qualified applicants
- ✅ **Document Verification Tools** — Streamline credential review
- 📣 **Communication Tools** — Notify applicants of updates and decisions

## 🗺️ Scope

- 📍 **Geographic Coverage:** Cebu City only
- 👥 **Target Users:** Incoming freshmen and currently enrolled undergraduate college students, and scholarship providers (universities, NGOs, LGUs)
- 🌐 **Platform:** Web-based (browser accessible, no app download required)
- 🎓 **Scholarship Types:** Institutional, government/LGU-funded, and private/NGO-sponsored

---

## ⛔ Limitations

- Does **not** handle financial disbursement or fund transfers
- Does **not** integrate with external government databases (CHED, DSWD)
- Requires a **stable internet connection** for full functionality
- Provider legitimacy is handled through **manual pre-screening** by system admins
- Limited to **Cebu City** — does not cover other regions
